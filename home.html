WHAT IS CORONAVIRUS?? WHAT IS COVID-19??

Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses which may cause illness in animals or humans. The most recently discovered coronavirus causes coronavirus disease COVID-19. This new virus and disease were unknown before the outbreak began in Wuhan, China, in December 2019. COVID-19 is now a pandemic affecting many countries globally.

WHAT ARE THE SYMPTOMS OF COVID-19??

The most common symptoms of COVID-19 are:

  • FEVER
  • DRY COUGH
  • RESTLESSNESS
  • HEADACHE
  • NASAL CONGESTION
  • SORE THROAT
  • LOSS OF TASTE OR SMELL
